AASTU STUDENTS APP

Our recent Work

‘AASTU STUDENT’ is multipurpose and very interactive android application designed for Addis Ababa Science and Technology University students to make them able to get different information about academics E-sources services that are available in the campus, clubs and school of excellences and more other additional features in one mobile application. These mobile applications aim to reduce sense of tumbles in new students joining the university (fresh students), give service and promote the college

     Academic
  • Class Schedule with widgets
  • GPA calculator
  • Department Information
  • Academic Calendar
  • Note taker
  • Student info and e-learning portal from www.aastu.edu.et and more..
